About Us

Your trusted partner in automotive excellence

Welcome to GREATMOON MOTOR SPARES

Established in 2015, GREATMOON MOTOR SPARES has been serving the automotive community of Zimbabwe with dedication and expertise. We are a leading supplier of genuine motor spares and auto parts in the Mutare region.

Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for vehicle owners, mechanics, and automotive businesses throughout the area. We understand that your vehicle is essential to your daily life, and we're here to keep it running smoothly.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, genuine motor spares at competitive prices while delivering exceptional customer service. We aim to be the most reliable source for auto parts in Zimbabwe, ensuring every customer finds exactly what they need.
